All About Vinora

Welcome to Vinora official web site. Vinora is a platform that provide services for retailers and delivery companies which provide fast consuming food products. Delivery companies that follow a itinerary management system can partner Up with us and experience the next level of ordering.

Vinora Mob

This is vinora mobile application for retailers. All the retailers are welcomed to use this application. Facilities like ordering products from any registered delivery company will be supplied through this and also don't forget to experience the offeres. Get downloaded this applicatioin and experience the next level of ordering.

Vinora Rep

This is Vinora mobile application for sales representatives of registered delivery companies. Sales represenatives will get the facility of viewing retail shop locations , their daily deliveries and provided chat service will give a chance to chat with retailers.
